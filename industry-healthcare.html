05 FAQ

Questions from healthcare teams.

Is it safe to use AI with patient data?

It can be, when designed correctly. We use approved data sources, EU-region or self-hosted models, strict access controls and human-in-the-loop review so AI assists without exposing or deciding on sensitive data.

Can you work with our existing patient management system?

In most cases yes — we integrate through APIs or secure data exchange. Where systems are closed, we build careful middleware rather than risky workarounds.

How do you handle GDPR and clinical data?

We design to GDPR Articles 5, 25 and 32 — data minimisation, privacy by design and security of processing — and document handling for governance review.

Want to cut admin without risking data?

Tell us where your team loses time. We'll show what's safe to automate — and how we'd protect every record.
