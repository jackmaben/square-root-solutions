08 FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Which cloud should we use?

We work across Azure, AWS and GCP. For Irish enterprises we often recommend Azure for its EU data-residency options and Microsoft ecosystem fit — but we choose based on your existing systems, compliance needs and budget.

Can you clean up our existing data mess?

Yes — that's a common starting point. We assess your sources, build reliable pipelines into a clean warehouse, and add validation so quality improves over time rather than degrading.

Do we need this before doing AI?

Often, yes. AI is only as good as its inputs. A clean, well-structured data layer dramatically improves the accuracy and reliability of AI integrations and RAG systems.

Can you reduce our cloud costs?

Usually. We audit usage, right-size resources, introduce autoscaling and add cost visibility. Many clients see meaningful monthly savings after optimisation.

Do you offer ongoing DevOps support?

Yes — we provide monitoring, maintenance and on-call support on retainer, or set your team up to run the platform with documentation and training.

Sitting on data you can't use?

Tell us about your systems and reporting pain. We'll map a path to clean data and reliable infrastructure.
