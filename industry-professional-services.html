05 FAQ

Questions from professional services firms.

Is client data safe with AI tools?

Yes, when built correctly. We ground AI in your approved, access-controlled knowledge, keep data in secure EU-region or self-hosted models where needed, and keep humans reviewing outputs — so confidentiality holds.

Can AI really help with document work?

For drafting, review and summarisation grounded in your templates and precedent, yes — substantially. We design it to assist your experts, who stay accountable for the final work.

Can you integrate with our practice management system?

In most cases yes — we connect through APIs or secure data exchange to the practice-management, document and time systems you already rely on.
