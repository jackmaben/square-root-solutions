05 FAQ

Questions from finance teams.

Can we trust AI with financial figures?

We design AI to assist, not to autonomously decide. Outputs are drawn from approved, traceable sources and reviewed by your team, so you keep accuracy and accountability.

Can you automate our reporting?

Yes — this is one of the highest-value engagements we deliver. We build validated pipelines that produce consistent reports automatically, eliminating manual spreadsheet work and its errors.

How do you handle security and compliance?

We build with strong access controls, encryption, audit logging and EU data residency, designed to GDPR and the scrutiny finance environments require.

Tired of rebuilding the same reports?

Show us your reporting and reconciliation pain. We'll map what we can automate — accurately and auditably.
