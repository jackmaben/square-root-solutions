01 The problem

Why so many apps quietly fail.

Most app problems aren't about ideas — they're about performance, connection and the gap between launch and real use.

1

Slow, janky experience

Laggy screens and clunky navigation that feel worse than the website, so users uninstall within a week.

2

Disconnected from your systems

An app that can't see your live inventory, orders or customer data because it was never properly integrated.

3

Breaks offline

Apps that fall apart the moment connectivity drops — useless for field, retail or travel use.

4

Stalls after launch

No analytics, no update path and no plan for the App Store review and release cycle.

08 FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Native or cross-platform — which should we choose?

It depends on your priorities. Cross-platform (React Native or Flutter) is faster and cheaper for most business apps. Native makes sense when you need maximum performance, heavy hardware use or platform-specific polish. We advise based on your case during discovery.

How much does a mobile app cost?

Most apps range from €25,000 for a focused MVP to €90,000+ for a full product with custom back-end. A discovery workshop produces a fixed-price proposal before development.

How long until launch?

A well-scoped MVP typically reaches the stores in 8–14 weeks. Larger products with complex back-ends take longer; we deliver in sprints so you see progress throughout.

Do you handle App Store and Play Store submission?

Yes — we manage the full submission and review process, set up automated release pipelines, and can maintain your store presence after launch.

Can the app work with our existing systems?

Yes. We build the APIs and integrations to connect your app to your CRM, inventory, payments and data, so it reflects live business information.

Have a mobile product in mind?

Tell us what it needs to do and who it's for. We'll propose the right platform, scope and timeline.
